IMPORTANCE YOUR DONATION

Your Help will help in Prevention and control of NCDs

In Prevention and control of NCDs we detect and mitigate the burden of NCDs as well as their management in the community of Rwanda.

Your donation will help to promote Adolescent, sexual reproductive health and right

URPHSA to together with HPO initiated Ishema Ryanjye Project which is under the program of Adolescents Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights. Its aim is to address problems of teenage pregnancy and sexual transmitted diseases through comprehensive sexuality education and behavior change communication.

Your help will help to eradicate the double burden of malnutrition

URPHSA to together with HPO initiated KUZA NEZA Project which is under the program of nutrition program. to protect maternal and child health in the community and strengthening school feeding program through nutrition sensitive and specific interventions to tackle malnutrition issues.

Your help may help us to Promote Environmental health and waste management

Our program seeks to provide fair, long-term, and cost-effective access to water and sanitation facilities and environmental safety to Rwandan communities.

Work With us to eradicate public health challenges

UR-PHSA also conduct community interventions with the aim of coming up with innovative solutions that will tackle the public health challenges they encounter in the respective communities and thus contribute to the accomplishment of certain SDGs in Rwanda by 2030.
